The Challenge

Many children struggle to focus on traditional educational materials. There's a need for interactive learning methods that turn the learning process into an enjoyable experience and stimulate children's natural curiosity.

My Role

I designed a colorful and engaging interface suitable for children while maintaining simplicity and ease of use. Developed educational games and interactive activities using Flutter Framework and integrated the Phaser game engine for complex interactive elements.
