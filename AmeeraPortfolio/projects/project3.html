FloraScan App

An AI-powered plant identification app for nature enthusiasts

Back to Projects View on GitHub

The Challenge

Nature enthusiasts and gardeners struggle to identify plant species and wildflowers. Existing solutions are either inaccurate or require constant internet connection, limiting their usefulness in outdoor excursions.

My Role

I designed an intuitive interface focused on easy photo capture and clear results display. Developed the plant recognition algorithm using TensorFlow Lite and optimized it to work on mobile devices without constant internet connection.
